Zulope

A salon booking platform built for customers and salon owners alike.

Zulope is a salon appointment booking platform that connects customers with salons, spas, and beauty parlors. It also gives makeup artists a place to showcase their portfolio — wedding looks and other major functions — so clients can discover and book with confidence.

Zulope — For Customers

  • Browse nearby salons, spas, and beauty parlors
  • View services, pricing, and real-time availability
  • Book appointments in seconds
  • Explore makeup artist portfolios for weddings and special occasions
  • Discover and book with confidence
