Privacy Policy & Terms

Zulope Salon Admin (Partner App) — Version 1.0

App: Zulope Salon Admin  |  Version: 1.0  |  Effective Date: April 11, 2026  |  Platform: Android

Privacy Policy

1. Age Requirement

  • This app is intended for users aged 13 years and above only.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • If we become aware that a user under 13 has provided personal data, we will promptly delete that information.

2. Information Collected

  • Store owner name, phone number, and address.
  • Stylist names, services offered, and pricing details.

3. Images & Media

  • Salon admins may upload store images and stylist photos.
  • Images are used to display salon profiles in the customer app.

4. Location Data

  • Location data is collected to display store location on maps.

5. Reviews & Ratings

  • Customer reviews and ratings are displayed for stores and stylists.

6. How We Use Your Data

  • Data is used for store listing, booking management, and platform operations.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Data may be shared with service providers (e.g., map services, cloud hosting) solely to operate the platform.
  • We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

7. Security & Retention

  • Reasonable security practices are followed to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for platform operations, compliance, and moderation purposes.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

8. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not share personal data with third parties except as required to provide the service (e.g., map display, cloud infrastructure).
  • We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users and platform.

9. Your Rights

  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time.
  • To exercise these rights, contact us at gunasekaran@thegksoft.com.

Terms & Conditions

1. Store Responsibilities

  • Salons must provide accurate and up-to-date information.
  • Fake or misleading information is not allowed.

2. Images & Authenticity

  • Uploaded images must accurately represent the salon and services.
  • Fake or misleading images are prohibited.

3. Reviews Policy

  • Salon admins must not manipulate or fake reviews.
  • Violations may lead to takedown.

4. Content Moderation & Takedown

  • We reserve the right to remove stores or content that violate policies.
  • Stores using fake information may be taken down.

5. Re-Application Rules

  • Removed stores may re-apply after correcting violations.
  • Repeated violations may lead to permanent removal.

6. Platform Rights

  • We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts for violations.

Contact

For questions regarding this policy, reach out at gunasekaran@thegksoft.com.
