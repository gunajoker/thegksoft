The techniques behind MemoryStash

Each method is centuries old, proven by memory champions, and now available without an internet connection.

PEG System

The PEG system (including the Major System) maps digits to consonant sounds, letting you convert any number into a memorable word or image. Once your PEG table is set, you can memorise PINs, dates, phone numbers, and ranked lists in seconds.

Memory Palace (Method of Loci)

Used by ancient Greek orators and modern memory champions alike, the Memory Palace places information at specific locations along a familiar mental route. Walking the route in your mind retrieves the information in perfect order.

Mnemonics

Mnemonics are memory shortcuts — acronyms, rhymes, keyword links, and story chains that tie new information to something you already know. MemoryStash helps you craft and store these links so review is instant.

Spaced Quizzing

Testing yourself is the most effective way to transfer information to long-term memory. MemoryStash quizzes draw from everything you've stored and flag the items you struggle with, so you spend time on what matters.
