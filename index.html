What is Zulope?

Zulope brings salon discovery and booking into one simple app. Find a salon you like, see who's available, check the services and prices up front, and lock in an appointment — all from your phone.

Booking a haircut or a grooming session shouldn't mean back and forth phone calls or guessing whether a slot is free. Zulope shows you real salons around you, their stylists, and the services they offer, so you can decide and book with confidence.

Once you book, the salon gets your request instantly and you receive booking updates over WhatsApp — so you always know where your appointment stands.

  1. Open Zulope and see salons near you on the map.
  2. Browse a salon's stylists, services, and pricing.
  3. Pick a stylist, a service, and a time that works.
  4. Confirm — and get updates on WhatsApp.
  5. Leave a review to help others choose well.

For salons — Zulope Partner

The companion app that lets salons and stylists manage everything behind the bookings.

Zulope Partner gives salons their own space to set up their profile, list stylists and services, and manage incoming bookings as they come in.

Walk-ins, schedules, and earnings all live in one place — so the team can focus on the chair, not the paperwork.

  • List stylists, services, and pricing.
  • Receive and manage bookings in real time.
  • Add manual bookings for walk-ins.
  • Track performance with built-in analytics.

Our vision

India's salon and grooming space is huge — and almost entirely offline. We're here to change that.

Across India, most salon bookings still happen by phone, by walk-in, or by hoping a chair is free. Schedules live in notebooks, regulars are remembered by memory, and customers have no easy way to compare salons or stylists before they show up. It's a massive, everyday market with very little technology behind it.

Zulope exists to close that gap — to bring the convenience of modern booking to neighbourhood salons everywhere, so customers book in seconds and salons run on something better than a notebook.

Go digital

Move salons off paper diaries and missed calls, onto a simple system that keeps bookings, stylists, and pricing in one place.

Make it convenient

Booking an appointment should be as easy as ordering food — pick, tap, done, with no phone tag and no uncertainty.

Grow local salons

Give neighbourhood salons visibility, fewer no-shows, and the insights they need to serve customers better and earn more.
